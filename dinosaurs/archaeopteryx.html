Yearly Omikuji Fortune

Love
Someone with questionable plumage and excellent timing will try to impress you via midair snack deliveries. Accept one, but critique their wing posture for dominance.
The Person You're Waiting For
A stargazing loner who writes cave haikus and folds leaves into hearts finally lands beside you during a windy dusk. You feel seen. Also slightly flattered. Possibly endangered.
Lost Item
Your favorite shiny beetle carcass, previously assumed stolen, is actually stuck between your tail feathers. It’s fossilizing nicely. Keep it—it’s trending.
Studies and Learning
You understand theory by reenacting it upside down from branches. Eventually, someone notices and declares you a thought leader. You nod sagely while nibbling a pinecone.
Work and Projects
You begin leading aerial scouting missions. No one understands the maps you draw with claw gestures, but everyone agrees they look majestic and imply success.
Taking a Trip
A spontaneous glide into unknown skies brings new friends and one awkward mid-air collision with a flirtatious Pterosaur. You recover gracefully and never speak of it again.
Wish
You wish for a peaceful perch. What you receive: a cliffside crevice with great acoustics, unpredictable breezes, and a fan club of ants who applaud your nesting style.
Health
Your wings thrive on dramatic gestures. Stretch often, preen responsibly, and avoid nesting near noisy volcanoes. Molting is inevitable—do it with flair and backup vocals.
